PHILHEALTH PROGRAMS

Philhealth Konsulta

Gsmci E-konsulta

The Konsulta Package is a primary care benefit that offers members access to essential health services, including doctor consultations, lab tests, and preventive care. It ensures timely medical attention, helping to detect health problems early and manage them effectively. Members can consult licensed doctors for checkups, chronic disease management, or treatment of common ailments, while diagnostic tests provide an accurate assessment of health risks. Preventive services, such as screenings and wellness counseling, promote healthy habits and lower the chance of future illness. By covering these important services, the Konsulta Package improves overall health, reduces medical costs, and supports a stronger, more accessible health care system, empowering individuals to take charge of their well-being, fostering a proactive approach to health, and encouraging regular monitoring for long-term wellness.
