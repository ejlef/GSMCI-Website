ABOUT US


Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc.(GSMCI), established in 2016, stands as the sole private hospital in the Province of Guimaras. This three-story facility boasts a 100-bed capacity and holds a Level 2 classification, making it the first and only private hospital of its kind in the province. (GSMCI) offers a comprehensive range of services, including hemodialysis, laboratory testing, pharmacy services, radiology, and a central supply room, ensuring a high level of care for its patients. Beyond its impressive infrastructure and services, (GSMCI)distinguishes itself through its dedicated team of medical professionals. The hospital is known for its exceptional doctors and nurses, who are committed to providing compassionate and skilled care. The clean and well-maintained facilities further enhance the patient experience, creating a comfortable and healing environment.

×

Vision

To provide affordable, quality healthcare and accommodation to the people of Guimaras and nearby provinces.

Mission

To be the most trusted and highest quality healthcare provider in the island of Guimaras and nearby provinces through affordable state-of-the-art equipment, quality accommodation, and competent personnel.

Goals

  • To provide the highest quality affordable medical care at the hospital and its community with utmost compassion and integrity.
  • To provide and actively participate in the continuing medical education of the physicians and its medical staff.
  • To provide and recommend ways to upgrade hospital services and facilities.
  • To promote harmonious and dynamic personal relationships among medical and hospital staff.

Core Values

G- Goal-oriented
S- Service of Excellence
M- Medically capacitated
