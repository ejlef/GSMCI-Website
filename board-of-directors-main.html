BOARD OF DIRECTORS

DR. RONELO P. BUÑOL CHAIRMAN OF THE BOARD

DR. RONELO P. BUÑOL joined Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. in October 2016. He is a General Surgeon and a Fellow of the Philippine College of Surgeons. He is also a Board Member of the Philippine Society of General Surgeons, Panay Chapter, and holds a Masteral Degree in Hospital Administration. Additionally, Dr. Buñol is a Visiting Consultant at Iloilo Mission Hospital, St. Paul’s Hospital, and Western Visayas Medical Center. Dr. Ronelo P. Buñol graduated with a BS in Biological Sciences from West Visayas State University, located in Lapaz, Iloilo City.

PUTLI JAHALLIA A. FUERTE, RN, MMHoA PRESIDENT

PUTLI JAHALLIA A. FUERTE is the President of Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. since October 2016. She is holding a Masteral Degree in Hospital Administration and currently taking up Doctor of Philosophy in Development Administration at Philippine Christian University. Mrs. Putli Jahallia A. Fuerte, is a Registered Nurse. She is a BS Nursing graduate from the Philippine Women’s University located in Taft Avenue, Manila.

DR. STEPHANIE A. ABELLO VICE PRESIDENT

DR. STEPHANIE A. ABELLO, apart from being a Vice President in Guimaras Specialist Medical Medical Center, Inc. Dr. Stephanie A. Abello holds a position as Chairperson in Department of Pathology at Western Visayas Medical Center, Inc. and a consultant in Department of Pa Pathology and Laboratory Medicine at Western Visayas Medical Center. Dr. Stephanie A. Abello, is a BS Medical Technologist graduate from the University of San Agustin located in General Luna St., Iloilo city.

DR. JENNIFER M. BUÑOL CORPORATE SECRETARY

DR. JENNIFER M. BUÑOL, joined Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. as Corporate Secretary in October 2016. She is part of Philippine Pediatric Society and has a Masteral Degree in Hospital Administration and was a Medical Officer III in Western Visayas Medical Center. Dr. Jennifer M. Buñol, is a BS Medical Technologist graduate from Centro Escolar University located in Mendiola St., San Miguel, Manila.

DR. SHARON S. ZAMBARRANO CORPORATE TREASURER

DR. SHARON S. ZAMBARRANO is a Corporate Treasurer of Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. from 2016 to date. She is currently part of Diabetes Philippines and Philippine College of Physician. Dr. Sharon S. Zambarrano, is a BS Medical Technologist graduate from the University of San Augustin located in General Luna St., Iloilo city.

ENGR. JUNGI R. FUERTE DIRECTOR

ENGR. JUNGI R. FUERTE joined Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. as Director since October 2016. He is a Founder of Pope John Paul Hospital in Alabang, Zapote and also a Founder of Divine Grace Medical Center in Cavite. Engr. Jungi R. Fuerte, is a BS Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of San Agustin Located in General Lune St.,Iloilo city.

DR. MARY HOPE I. GALLEGO DIRECTOR

DR. MARY HOPE I. GALLEGO joined Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. as Independent Director August 2024. She earned her Diplomate in Pediatric last March 2023 and part of Philippine Pediatric Society. Dr. Mary Hope I. Gallego, is a BS Science in Biology graduate from the West Visayas State University located in Lapaz, Iloilo city.

DR. RAEL A. DULLANO INDEPENDENT DIRECTOR

DR. RAEL A. DULLANO, joined Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. as Independent Director August 2024. He was 2nd placer in Medical Technologist Board Exam and passed 4 professional examination (MedTech Licensure Exam, Medical Licensure Exam, Philippine Specialty Board of Internal Medicine and Philippine Subspecialty Board of Allergy Asthma & Immunology). He is currently part of Philippine Medical Association, Philippine College of Physicians and Philippine Society of Allergy Asthma and Immunology. Dr. Rael A. Dullano, is a BS Medical Technologist graduate from the Central Philippine University located in Jaro, Iloilo city.
