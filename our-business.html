OUR BUSINESS


Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc.(GSMCI) stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence in the Province of Guimaras. This private hospital, established in 2016, serves as the sole provider of comprehensive medical services for the Guimarasnon. Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. (GSMCI) is dedicated to delivering high-quality care in a comfortable and compassionate environment, ensuring the well-being of the community it serves. Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. (GSMCI) commitment to providing excellent healthcare is evident in its comprehensive range of services. The hospital offers a wide array of medical specialties, including:
- Hemodialysis: Providing vital treatment for patients with kidney failure.
- Laboratory Testing: Ensuring accurate diagnoses and monitoring patient health.
- Pharmacy Services: Dispensing medications and providing expert advice on medication management.
- Radiology: Utilizing advanced imaging techniques to diagnose and treat medical conditions.
- Central Supply Room: Maintaining a sterile and well-stocked environment for medical supplies.
Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. (GSMCI) dedication to patient care extends beyond its facilities and services. The hospital boasts a team of highly skilled and compassionate medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and support staff, who are committed to providing personalized and attentive care. The hospital's clean and modern environment further enhances the patient experience, creating a comfortable and healing atmosphere. Guimaras Specialist Medical Center, Inc. (GSMCI) commitment to providing accessible and quality healthcare makes it an invaluable asset to the community of Guimaras. The hospital's dedication to its patients, coupled with its advanced facilities and skilled staff, ensures that residents have access to the best possible medical care.
